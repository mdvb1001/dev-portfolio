About

Actor turned software developer, Max is eager to pursue his artistic endeavours by learning from and collaborating with a vabriant and ambitious team. Max was born in Brussels, Belgium, to an American mother and Belgian father. He grew up on two continents and is completely bilingual French-English.

Having shared his adult life in New York, LA and Chicago, Max has a unique vision on design and aesthetic that comes only with experiencing a wide variety of places and cultures.

He currently works with JavaScript, NodeJS, Express, CSS, HTML, Handlebars, SQL, Mongoose, React and Bootstap. Below are few examples of the apps he developped while honing his skills at the Northwestern Coding Boot Camp.
